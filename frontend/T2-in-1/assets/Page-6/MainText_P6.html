DEVELOPING IMPROVED CROPS

The scientists of the Oficina de Estudios Especiales (OEE) recognized the urgency of developing new and improved varieties to curb food shortages associated with population growth. They initiated research, using varieties from throughout the country and worldwide for crop improvement.

This required meticulous collecting, cataloguing, cultivation and crossing of plants. Research was also subject to time, logistics and funding constraints with feedback from farmers required to validate their efforts. Eventually these efforts began to bear fruits. By applying technologies like modern irrigation methods, pesticides and fertilizers, higher yielding varieties were successfully bred and grown by farmers.
